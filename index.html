Privacy Policy

StatusSaverPro is developed by an independent developer to help users save WhatsApp statuses, create stylish text, and generate multi-line text content. Your privacy is very important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

Information We Collect

StatusSaverPro does not collect, store, or share any personal information or media files. All data (images, videos, text) remain stored locally on your device.

How the App Works

- Status Saver: Allows you to view and save WhatsApp statuses (images and videos) from your device storage. - Textify: Lets you style and decorate your text with creative fonts and symbols. - Loopify: Helps you repeat or format text into multiple lines quickly.

Permissions Used

- Storage Access: Required only to read and save media from your WhatsApp status folder. - No Internet data is collected or transmitted from your device.

Third-Party Services

StatusSaverPro does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.

Data Security

Since all files and data are stored locally on your device, we do not have access to your personal information in any way.
